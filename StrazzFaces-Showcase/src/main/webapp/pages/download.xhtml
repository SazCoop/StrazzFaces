Downloading with Maven

To download StrazzFaces with maven add the following repository and dependency to your pom.xml 

<repositories>
	<repository>
		<id>strazzfaces</id>
		<name>StrazzFaces</name>
		<url>http://www.francescostrazzullo.info/blog/maven/</url>
	</repository>
</repositories>
<dependencies>
	<dependency>
		<groupId>it.strazz.faces</groupId>
		<artifactId>StrazzFaces</artifactId>
		<version>0.1</version>
	</dependency>
</dependencies>
